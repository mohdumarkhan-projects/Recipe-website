\## Ingredients

\- Meat (Gosht)

\- Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1.5 tbsp

\- Fried Onions (Birista): 1/2 cup

\- Turmeric (Haldi): 1/2 tsp

\- Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder: 1 tsp

\- Spicy Red Chilli Powder: 1 tsp

\- Coriander Powder: 1 tsp

\- Salt: 1/2 tbsp

\- Green Chillies: 4

\- Tomatoes: 2

\- Black Masoor Daal: 1 cup



\## Steps

1\. Clean, wash meat well, and drain water

2\. Add ginger-garlic paste to meat

3\. Add fried onions, turmeric, Kashmiri red chilli powder, spicy red chilli powder, coriander powder, and salt

4\. Add 4 green chillies (broken)

5\. Add 2 tomatoes (cut and mixed in)

6\. Mix all ingredients well with hands and place in refrigerator

7\. Take out black masoor daal and keep ready

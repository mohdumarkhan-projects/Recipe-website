\## slug: pressure-cooking-palak

title: Pressure Cooking Palak

image: pressure-cooking-palak.jpg



\## Ingredients



\* 2 bunches (gaddi) Spinach (Palak)



\## Steps



1\. Place the spinach in a pressure cooker.

2\. Cook for 1 whistle.

3\. Release the pressure immediately and turn off the gas.

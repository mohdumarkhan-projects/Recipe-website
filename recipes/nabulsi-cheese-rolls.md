\## slug: nabulsi-cheese-rolls

title: Nabulsi Cheese Rolls

image: nabulsi-cheese-rolls.jpg



\## Ingredients



\* Cheese (cut into long pieces)

\* Bread slices (crusts removed)

\* Salt

\* Black pepper

\* Egg

\* Bread crumbs



\## Steps



1\. Cut the cheese into long strips.

2\. Remove the crusts from the bread slices and flatten them thoroughly with a rolling pin.

3\. Place a piece of cheese inside the flattened bread.

4\. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and black pepper over the cheese.

5\. Roll the bread tightly.

6\. Dip the roll into beaten egg and coat thoroughly with bread crumbs.

7\. Semi-deep fry until golden.

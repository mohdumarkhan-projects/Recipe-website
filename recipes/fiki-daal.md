\## Ingredients

\- Tur Daal (Split Pigeon Pea)

\- Turmeric: 1/2 tsp

\- Cumin: 1/2 tsp

\- Garlic: 8 cloves

\- Salt

\- Tomato: 1 small, chopped

\- Green Chillies: 2, chopped

\- Coriander

\- Mustard Seeds

\- Cumin Seeds

\- Curry Leaves

\- Whole Black Peppercorns

\- Ghee



\## Steps

1\. Cook Tur Daal with turmeric, cumin, and 8 cloves of garlic. Take 3 whistles in the cooker.

2\. Mash the lentils, add water as needed and salt, and bring to a boil.

3\. Stir in one small chopped tomato, 2 chopped green chilies, and some coriander for 2 minutes.

4\. Temper with mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, and whole black peppercorns in ghee.

\## Ingredients

\- Mutton

\- Water: Half cup

\- Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tablespoon

\- Turmeric: 1 teaspoon

\- Onion: 1, sliced

\- Oil: Half cup

\- Tomatoes: 5, sliced

\- Green chillies: 5

\- Coriander/mint

\- Kashmiri Lal Mirch: 1 tablespoon

\- Tikhi Lal Mirch: 1 tablespoon

\- Dhaniya ki Bukki: Half tablespoon

\- Potatoes: 3 large, cut into 6 pieces

\- Zeera Powder (Cumin): 1 tablespoon

\- Ghee: 4 tablespoons

\- Hot water: Slightly more than 2 cups

\- Garam Masala: 1.5 teaspoons



\## Steps

1\. Wash the mutton.

2\. Add half cup water, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, and sliced onion to a large cooker.

3\. Take 1 whistle.

4\. Open the cooker and add oil, tomatoes, green chillies, and coriander/mint.

5\. Add powdered spices: Kashmiri Lal Mirch, Tikhi Lal Mirch, and Dhaniya ki Bukki.

6\. Saute on high heat for 2 minutes.

7\. Take 1 more whistle.

8\. Check if the meat is done. If not, take one more whistle.

9\. Saute well on medium heat for 5 minutes.

10\. Add potatoes and Zeera Powder.

11\. Add Ghee.

12\. After 5 minutes of saute-ing, add hot water.

13\. Add Garam Masala and put on slow heat for 5 minutes.

14\. Serve with steam rice and kachumbar (tomato, onion, salt, lemon salad).

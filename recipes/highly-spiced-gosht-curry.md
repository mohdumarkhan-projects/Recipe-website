\## Ingredients

\- Gosht (Meat)

\- Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tbsp

\- Birista (Fried Onion): 1/2 cup

\- Turmeric: 1 tsp

\- Kashmiri Red Chili Powder: 3 tsp

\- Hot Red Chili Powder: 1 tsp

\- Coriander Powder: 2 tsp

\- Salt: 1 tbsp

\- Mustard Oil: 1/2 cup

\- Cumin Powder: 1 big tbsp

\- Tomatoes: 5

\- Potatoes: 3

\- Fresh Coriander: 5 tbsp

\- Mint



\## Steps

1\. Wash the gosht thoroughly; ensure no water remains.

2\. In a large pressure cooker, combine the gosht, all the masalas, ginger-garlic paste, birista, salt, mustard oil, and tomatoes.

3\. Mix all ingredients thoroughly by hand.

4\. Take 1 pressure cooker whistle.

5\. Peel and cut 3 potatoes each into 2 pieces; keep in water.

6\. Cut 5 tomatoes into round slices.

7\. Chop 5 tbsp of fresh coriander and keep ready.

8\. Tear the mint leaves by hand.

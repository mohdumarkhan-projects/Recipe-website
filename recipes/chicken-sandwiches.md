\## Ingredients



\* Chicken Breast

\* Bread (8 slices)

\* Ketchup

\* Bell Pepper

\* Fries (for serving)



\## Steps



1\. Prepare the chicken breast and slice the bell peppers.

2\. Assemble 4 sandwiches using 8 slices of bread and the prepared chicken and peppers.

3\. Serve the sandwiches with fries and ketchup.



\## Notes



This recipe was designed as part of an Iftar spread including leftover Beef Mandi, fruits, and Rooh Afza.

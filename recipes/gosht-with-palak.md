\## Ingredients

\- Gosht (Meat)

\- Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tbsp

\- Onion: 2 medium

\- Salt: 1/2 tbsp

\- Palak (Spinach)

\- Green Chilies: 8

\- Tomato: 1



\## Steps

1\. In a pressure cooker, combine the gosht, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 2 medium onions, and 1/2 tbsp salt.

2\. Take 1 pressure cooker whistle.

3\. Prepare the rest of the ingredients while the meat cooks.

4\. Keep spinach in water to prevent discoloration.

5\. Coarsely crush 8 green chilies.

6\. Chop 1 tomato.

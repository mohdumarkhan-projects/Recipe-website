\## Ingredients

\- Aalu (Potato)

\- Matter (Peas)

\- Gobhi (Cauliflower)

\- Oil

\- Ghee: 2 tbsp

\- Onion: 2

\- Tomato: 1

\- Ginger

\- Garlic

\- Green Chili

\- Fresh Coriander

\- Dry Coconut: 1/2 cup

\- Cumin: A pinch

\- Turmeric

\- Tikhi Lal Mirch

\- Kashmiri Lal Mirch

\- Coriander Powder: 1 tbsp

\- Salt

\- Dahi (Yogurt): 1/2 cup

\- Hot Water

\- Garam Masala Powder: A pinch



\## Steps

1\. Wash vegetables thoroughly and keep in a sieve.

2\. Heat oil and 2 tbsp ghee together.

3\. Fry the vegetables one by one for 2 minutes each and set aside.

4\. In a grinder, combine onions, tomato, ginger, garlic, green chilies, fresh coriander, and dry coconut. Grind to a paste.

5\. In the same oil, add a pinch of cumin.

6\. Add the ground masala paste.

7\. Add dry spices, salt, and whisked yogurt.

8\. Saut√© for 5 minutes.

9\. Add the fried vegetables to the masala.

10\. Add hot water to create desired gravy consistency.

11\. Add a pinch of garam masala powder.

12\. Cover and let it simmer for 5 minutes, then turn off the gas.

## Ingredients
- Mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli) 2 cups
- Tofu or chicken 200g
- Soy sauce 3 tbsp
- Garlic 3 cloves
- Ginger 1 inch
- Oil 2 tbsp

## Steps
1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan
2. Add minced garlic and ginger, saut√© for 1 minute
3. Add protein (tofu/chicken) and cook until done
4. Add vegetables and stir fry for 5-7 minutes
5. Add soy sauce and mix well
6. Serve hot with rice

## Notes
Great for meal prep. Add chili flakes for spice.

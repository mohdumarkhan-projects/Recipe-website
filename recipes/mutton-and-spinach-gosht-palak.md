\## Ingredients

\- Mutton (Gosht)

\- Oil: Half cup

\- Spinach (Palak): One whole bunch, chopped

\- Green Chillies: 8, coarsely crushed

\- Coriander: 1 cup, chopped

\- Turmeric: 1 pinch

\- Salt: Half tablespoon

\- Tomato: 1 small, chopped

\- Garam Masala: 1 teaspoon



\## Steps

1\. Put all ingredients except Garam Masala into the cooker and place on medium heat.

2\. Take 2 whistles.

3\. After opening, check if the meat is tender. If not, take 1 more whistle.

4\. Once the meat is tender, saute well for 5 minutes.

5\. Add 1 teaspoon of Garam Masala.

6\. Saute for another 2 minutes.

7\. Give 2 minutes of dum on closed gas.

8\. Ready to serve.

\## Ingredients

\- Capsicum: 2 pieces

\- Onion: 1

\- Ginger (Adrak): A small amount

\- Cornflour

\- Soya Sauce

\- Vinegar

\- Green Chillies (Hari Mirch)



\## Steps

1\. Prepare ingredients as listed

2\. Clean and cut capsicum and onion

3\. Prepare ginger and green chillies

4\. Mix cornflour, soya sauce, and vinegar for the sauce

5\. Cook chicken with prepared ingredients

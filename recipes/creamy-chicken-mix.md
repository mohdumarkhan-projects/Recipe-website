\## slug: creamy-chicken-mix

title: Creamy Chicken Mix

image: creamy-chicken-mix.jpg



\## Ingredients



\* Chicken pieces

\* ¾ cup Mayonnaise

\* ½ tablespoon Honey

\* 2 tablespoons Ketchup

\* 1 tablespoon Schezwan Chutney

\* 1 tablespoon Sweet Chili Sauce

\* ½ tablespoon Paprika



\## Steps



1\. Fry the chicken pieces completely and set aside.

2\. In a bowl, combine mayonnaise, honey, ketchup, schezwan chutney, sweet chili sauce, and paprika.

3\. Mix the sauce well.

4\. Add the fried chicken into the sauce and mix thoroughly.

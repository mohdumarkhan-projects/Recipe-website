\## Ingredients

\- Grated Coconut: 1/2 cup

\- Black Peppercorns: 4

\- Cumin Seeds: 1/2 tsp

\- Garlic: 4 cloves

\- Green Chillies: 2

\- Coriander

\- Onion: 1 medium, chopped

\- Prawns

\- Tomato: 1, chopped

\- Turmeric: 1/2 tsp

\- Kashmiri Red Chili Powder: 1 tbsp

\- Coriander Powder: 1/2 tbsp

\- Green Chillies: 4, slit

\- Salt



\## Steps

1\. Grind 1/2 cup grated coconut, 4 black peppercorns, 1/2 tsp cumin seeds, 4 cloves of garlic, 2 green chilies, and some coriander until smooth.

2\. In an earthen pot, sauté 1 medium chopped onion for 5 minutes until translucent.

3\. Add the Prawns and sauté for 2 minutes.

4\. Add the ground paste, 1 chopped tomato, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tbsp Kashmiri red chili powder, 1/2 tbsp coriander powder, and 4 slit green chilies.

5\. Sauté for 2 minutes.

6\. Add salt as per taste, and let it cook on slow heat for 10 minutes.

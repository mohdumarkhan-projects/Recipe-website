\## Ingredients

\- Oil: Half cup minus 5 tablespoons

\- Ghee: 5 tablespoons

\- Shahzeera: 1 teaspoon

\- Dal Chini (Cinnamon): 1 piece

\- Laung (Cloves): 4

\- Kalimiri (Black Peppercorns): 6

\- Badi Ilaichi (Black Cardamom): 2

\- Birista (fried onion): Half cup

\- Tomatoes: 4, sliced

\- Hari mirchi: 6, slit

\- Podina (mint): A little

\- Kothmir (coriander): Half cup, chopped

\- Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves): 1 tablespoon

\- Adrak Lehsun (Ginger-Garlic Paste): 1 tablespoon

\- Kashmiri Lal Mirch: 2 tablespoons

\- Dhaniya ki Bukki (Coriander Powder): 1 tablespoon

\- Haldi (Turmeric): Less than half tablespoon

\- Prawns

\- Rice

\- Salt: Half tablespoon

\- Hot water



\## Steps

1\. Heat oil and ghee together.

2\. Add Shahzeera, Dal Chini, Laung, Kalimiri, and Badi Ilaichi.

3\. After 1 minute, add Birista.

4\. Add tomatoes, hari mirchi, Podina, Kothmir, and Kasuri Methi.

5\. Add Adrak Lehsun paste.

6\. Add powdered spices: Kashmiri Lal Mirch, Dhaniya ki Bukki, and Haldi.

7\. Saute all spices well on high heat for 5 minutes.

8\. Add the Prawns and saute for 5 minutes.

9\. Cover the lid and let it rest for 2 minutes.

10\. Add Rice and Salt, mix well.

11\. If you used 2 cups of rice, add slightly more than 2 cups of hot water. Mix and check the salt.

12\. Let it cook on medium heat.

13\. When the water begins to dry up, put it on low heat to steam.

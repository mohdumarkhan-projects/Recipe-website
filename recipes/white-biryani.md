\## Ingredients

\- Meat (Mutton/Beef)

\- Ginger

\- Garlic

\- Salt

\- Birista (Fried Onion): 1 cup

\- Whole Garam Masala: 4 cloves, 8 black peppercorns, 2-inch cinnamon, 1/2 tsp shahzeera

\- Oil: 1/2 cup

\- Cashews: 8 soaked

\- Almonds: 8 soaked

\- Yogurt (Dahi): 200g

\- Dried Apricots (Zardalu)

\- Whole Cashews

\- Green Cardamoms: 4

\- Black Cardamoms: 2

\- Nutmeg (Jaifal): Small piece

\- Cream: 1/2 cup

\- Mayonnaise: 1/2 cup

\- Potatoes (Optional)

\- Ghee: 4 tablespoons + 2 tablespoons

\- Rice

\- Shahzeera: 1/2 tsp

\- Oil: 1 tablespoon

\- Water: 1 tablespoon

\- Biryani Attar (Essence)

\- Kewra or Rose Water: 1/2 tsp



\## Steps

1\. Cook the meat with ginger, garlic, and salt until tender.

2\. Add 1 cup of birista and whole garam masala to the meat.

3\. Add 1/2 cup oil and saut√© for 5 minutes. Turn off gas and cover.

4\. Make a paste of 8 soaked cashews and 8 soaked almonds.

5\. Mix the paste with 200g yogurt, dried apricots, and some whole cashews.

6\. Grind 4 green cardamoms, 2 black cardamoms, and a small piece of nutmeg into a fine powder and add to the mixture.

7\. Add 1/2 cup cream and 1/2 cup mayonnaise. Add salt if needed.

8\. If using potatoes, fry them and mix into the meat mixture.

9\. Cook the entire mixture on slow gas flame for 10 minutes with 4 tablespoons of ghee, covered (stir once or twice).

10\. Cook rice separately, adding 1/2 tsp shahzeera to the water.

11\. Grease a pot with 1 tbsp oil and 1 tbsp water.

12\. Place a thin layer of rice in the pot.

13\. Add the entire meat mixture and spread it out.

14\. Cover with the remaining rice.

15\. Sprinkle with biryani attar and kewra or rose water.

16\. Pour 2 tbsp ghee on top and seal for final cooking.



\## Notes

\- For chicken, cook it with ginger, garlic, salt, and 1/2 cup water on "dam" first before following the rest of the recipe.

\- Do not use saffron in this recipe.

\## Ingredients

\- Chicken: Half chicken

\- Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tbsp

\- Lemon Juice: 1

\- Salt: 1/2 tbsp

\- Frying Masala: 6 tbsp

\- Tamarind: Half a lemon-sized amount

\- Peanuts (Sheng dana)

\- Coriander

\- Mint (Podina)

\- Green Chillies

\- Basmati Rice: 2 cups

\- Moong or Masoor Daal: 1/2 cup

\- Onion: 1, sliced



\## Steps

1\. Mix chicken with ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, salt, and frying masala

2\. Cover chicken and place in refrigerator

3\. Soak tamarind in a small bowl of water

4\. Shell peanuts and set aside

5\. Take out coriander, mint, and green chillies

6\. Wash rice and daal thoroughly and soak them

7\. Slice onion and keep aside

\## Ingredients

\- Gosht (Meat)

\- Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tbsp

\- Salt: 1/2 tbsp

\- Coconut Oil: 5 tbsp

\- Mustard Seeds: 1/2 tsp

\- Curry Leaves

\- Onion: 2

\- Tomato: 1

\- Fresh Coriander

\- Green Chilies: 6

\- Kashmiri Red Chili Powder: 1 1/2 tbsp

\- Turmeric: 1/2 tsp

\- Coriander Powder: 1 tbsp

\- Black Pepper Powder: 1 tsp

\- Garam Masala Powder: 1 tsp

\- Cumin Powder: 1 tsp

\- White Vinegar: 1 tbsp



\## Steps

1\. In a pressure cooker, combine the gosht (without water), 1/2 tbsp salt, and 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste.

2\. Take 3 pressure cooker whistles until the meat is fully tender.

3\. In a cooking pot, heat 5 tbsp coconut oil.

4\. Add 1/2 tsp mustard seeds and some curry leaves.

5\. Add 2 chopped onions, 1 chopped tomato, chopped coriander, and 6 slit green chilies. Sauté for 5 minutes.

6\. Add all the spice powders. Sauté thoroughly for 5 minutes.

7\. Add the cooked gosht and 1 tbsp white vinegar. Mix well.

8\. Cover and let it simmer for 10 minutes.

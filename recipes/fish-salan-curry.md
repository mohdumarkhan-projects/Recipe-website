\## Ingredients

\- Onion: 1 small

\- Grated Coconut: 1 cup

\- Black Peppercorns: 4

\- Cumin: 2 pinch

\- Tomato: 2

\- Fresh Coriander

\- Green Chili: 1

\- Kashmiri Lal Mirch: 1 tbsp

\- Tikhi Lal Mirch: 1 tbsp

\- Coriander Powder: 1/2 tbsp

\- Turmeric: 1/4 tbsp

\- Water



\## Steps

1\. Peel and cut onion into 4 pieces.

2\. Cut tomatoes into 4 pieces.

3\. Tear fresh coriander.

4\. In a mixer jar, combine onion, grated coconut, black peppercorns, cumin, tomatoes, fresh coriander, and green chili.

5\. Add all dry spices to the mixer.

6\. Add a little water and grind to a smooth paste.

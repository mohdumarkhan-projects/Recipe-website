\## Ingredients

\- Prawns

\- Onions: 4 medium, sliced

\- Tomato: 1, sliced

\- Green Chillies: 4, slit

\- Coriander (Kothmir): 4 tablespoons, chopped

\- Potatoes: 2 medium or 1 big, cut into small cubes

\- Rice: 2.5 cups



\## Steps

1\. Clean prawns and place in the refrigerator

2\. Slice 4 medium onions

3\. Slice 1 tomato

4\. Slit 4 green chillies

5\. Chop 4 tablespoons of coriander

6\. Cut potatoes into small cubes and keep in water

7\. Cook 2.5 cups of plain rice in a rice cooker

8\. Complete all cutting and chopping

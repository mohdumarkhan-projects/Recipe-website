\## Ingredients

\- Chicken

\- Whole Coriander (Akhkha Dhaniya)

\- Fennel Seeds (Sonf)

\- Onion: 1, cut into 4 pieces

\- Whole Garlic: 1, unpeeled

\- Whole Spices: 2 Bay Leaves, 6 Cloves, 8 Black Peppercorns, 1 Star Anise, 1 Cinnamon Stick, 1 tablespoon Cumin, Small \& Large Cardamoms

\- Water: Maximum 500 ml

\- Salt: 1 tablespoon

\- Ghee/Oil Mix

\- Onion: 1 large, sliced

\- Tomato: 1 small

\- Yogurt (Dahi): Half cup

\- Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tablespoon

\- Green Chilies: 8

\- Rice

\- Garam Masala Powder: Half teaspoon



\## Steps

1\. Combine chicken, whole coriander, fennel seeds, 4 pieces of onion, whole unpeeled garlic, water, and 1 tablespoon of salt. Put whole spices in a small cloth bag and add to the pot.

2\. Cook in a pressure cooker for 1 whistle or in a regular pot for 10 minutes.

3\. In the pulao pot, heat ghee/oil mix. Sauté 1 large sliced onion until translucent.

4\. Remove the whole spice potli and discard. Add boiled chicken to the pot and sauté with 1 small tomato, half a cup of yogurt, 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies for 5 minutes on a fast flame.

5\. Pour chicken stock into the pot. Once it comes to a good boil, add washed rice. Mix well. If stock isn't enough, add a little hot water.

6\. When rice is 90% cooked and ready for dum, add half a teaspoon of garam masala powder and mix.

7\. Cover pot with foil and place on a slow flame to cook through.

\## Ingredients

\- Daal

\- Spinach: Chopped

\- Tomatoes: 2

\- Green Chillies: 3

\- Onion: 1 small

\- Coriander: A little

\- Tamarind: A little

\- Turmeric: 1/2 teaspoon

\- Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder: 1/2 teaspoon

\- Asafoetida: 2 pinches

\- Salt: 1/2 tablespoon

\- Mustard Seeds

\- Cumin Seeds: total 1 teaspoon of both

\- Dry Red Chillies: 2 pieces

\- Curry Leaves

\- Oil: 4 tablespoons



\## Steps

1\. Add all ingredients (except salt) to pressure cooker

2\. Add enough water (half cup or as needed)

3\. Pressure cook for 2 whistles

4\. After pressure releases, mash/whisk the daal thoroughly

5\. Add salt and enough water to get desired consistency, then bring to a boil

6\. Heat 4 tablespoons of oil

7\. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chillies, and curry leaves to hot oil

8\. Pour tempering over boiling daal

9\. Serve hot

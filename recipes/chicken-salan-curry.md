\## Ingredients

\- Chicken

\- Desiccated Coconut: 4 tablespoons

\- Birista (Fried Onions): Half cup

\- Green Chilies: 2

\- Coriander (Kothmir): Half cup

\- Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 tablespoons

\- Salt: Half teaspoon

\- Tomatoes: 2 medium

\- Kashmiri Red Chili Powder: 1 tablespoon

\- Hot Red Chili Powder: Half tablespoon

\- Turmeric: Half teaspoon

\- Coriander Powder: 1 tablespoon

\- Ghee (Clarified Butter): 5 tablespoons

\- Garam Masala Powder: 1 teaspoon

\- Cumin Powder: 1 tablespoon

\- Hot Water: 2 cups

\- Mustard Oil



\## Steps

1\. Grind desiccated coconut, birista, green chilies, coriander, ginger-garlic paste, and salt into a fine paste.

2\. Heat mustard oil in a pot. Add washed chicken and sear for 5 minutes on a fast flame, turning the pieces.

3\. Add the entire ground paste to the chicken. Add 1 teaspoon of salt. Grind tomatoes into a puree and add to the pot.

4\. Add Kashmiri chili, hot chili, turmeric, and coriander powder. Mix well. Cover and cook for 5 minutes on a slow flame.

5\. After 5 minutes, open lid, stir well, add 5 tablespoons of ghee, and saut√© for 2 minutes.

6\. Add 2 cups of hot water. Let it simmer for 10 minutes on a slow flame with lid slightly ajar.

7\. After 10 minutes, add 1 teaspoon of garam masala and 1 tablespoon of cumin powder. Mix thoroughly.

8\. Cover pot completely, turn off gas, and let it sit. The curry is ready.

\## slug: kachche-kabab

title: Kachche Kabab (Raw Mince Kebabs)

image: kachche-kabab.jpg



\## Ingredients



\* Mince (Keema)

\* 1/2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste

\* Salt to taste

\* 1/2 tbsp Chilli flakes

\* 1/4 tsp Garam Masala

\* 1/4 tsp Cumin powder (Zeera powder)

\* 1/2 tbsp Coriander powder (Dhania powder)

\* 2 tbsp Butter

\* Chopped Onion

\* Chopped Coriander (Kothmir)

\* Chopped Mint (Podina)

\* 2 Chopped Green Chilies (Hari Mirchi)

\* 3 tbsp Besan (Gram flour/Pakora batter)

\* 1 Egg (optional)



\## Steps



1\. Combine mince, ginger-garlic paste, salt, chilli flakes, garam masala, cumin powder, and coriander powder in a mixer.

2\. Blend the mixture for 3 minutes.

3\. Add butter, chopped onion, coriander, mint, green chilies, and besan.

4\. Add one egg if using and mix the entire mixture well.

5\. Form the mixture into small kebabs.

6\. Heat oil in a pan and semi-deep fry on medium to low heat.



\## Notes



Serve with green chutney.

\## slug: bagara-chawal

title: Bagara Chawal (Spiced Rice)

image: bagara-chawal.jpg



\## Ingredients



\* Rice (soaked)

\* 3 tablespoons Oil

\* 5 tablespoons Ghee

\* 1 small Onion (thinly sliced)

\* 2 pinches Cumin seeds (Zeera)

\* 2 Green chilies (slit)

\* Coriander (Kothmir)

\* 8 leaves Mint (Podina)

\* Whole Garam Masalas

\* ½ teaspoon Ginger-Garlic Paste

\* Water

\* ½ tablespoon Salt



\## Steps



1\. Soak the rice and prepare all chopped ingredients.

2\. Heat 3 tablespoons oil and 5 tablespoons ghee in a pressure cooker.

3\. Sauté the thinly sliced onion until translucent.

4\. Add whole garam masala and half teaspoon ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.

5\. Stir in the mint, coriander, and green chilies.

6\. Add the soaked rice and mix well.

7\. Add water and half tablespoon salt and stir.

8\. Close the cooker and cook for 2 whistles.

9\. Release the pressure manually and turn off the gas.

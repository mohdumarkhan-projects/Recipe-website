\## slug: chicken-sandwich-wrap

title: Chicken Sandwich/Wrap

image: chicken-sandwich-wrap.jpg



\## Ingredients



\* Chicken fillet

\* Olive oil

\* 2 tablespoons Mayonnaise

\* 1 tablespoon Thousand Island dressing

\* 1 tablespoon Hot Chili Sauce

\* Cucumber

\* Lettuce

\* Tomato

\* Bread slices or wrap



\## Steps



1\. Fry the chicken fillet with a little olive oil.

2\. Coarsely chop the cooked chicken and set aside.

3\. Mix 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, 1 tablespoon thousand island, and 1 tablespoon hot chili sauce to create the sauce.

4\. Slice the cucumber, lettuce, and tomato.

5\. Spread the sauce onto a slice of bread or wrap.

6\. Layer with a lettuce leaf, the chopped chicken, cucumber, and tomato.

7\. Add an additional layer of sauce and top with the second slice of bread.

8\. Toast the sandwich or serve as a wrap.

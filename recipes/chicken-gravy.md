\## Ingredients

\- Oil: 4 tbsp

\- Tomatoes: 2

\- Onion: 1

\- Coriander (kothmir)

\- Mint (podina) leaves: 8

\- Green Chillies: 2

\- Chicken

\- Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tbsp

\- Kashmiri Chilli Powder: 2 tsp

\- Spicy Chilli Powder: 1 tsp

\- Coriander Powder: 1 tsp

\- Salt: 1/2 tbsp

\- Ghee: 2 tbsp

\- Potato: 1

\- Hot water: 1 to 1.5 cups

\- Garam Masala: 1 tsp

\- Cumin Powder: 1 tsp



\## Steps

1\. In clay pot, add 4 tbsp oil

2\. Add 2 tomatoes (cut into 4 pieces each) and 1 onion (thickly sliced)

3\. Fry for 5 minutes and take them out

4\. Grind fried onion and tomato with coriander, 8 mint leaves, and 2 green chillies

5\. In same pot, add less than 1/2 cup oil and fry chicken on high heat for 5 minutes

6\. Add ground paste, ginger-garlic paste, Kashmiri chilli powder, spicy chilli powder, coriander powder, and salt

7\. Braise mixture well for 5 minutes

8\. Add 2 tbsp ghee and 1 potato (cut into 4 pieces)

9\. Braise this mixture as well

10\. Add 1 to 1.5 cups of hot water

11\. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes

12\. Reduce to slow heat and cook with lid for 5 minutes

13\. Add 1 tsp garam masala and 1 tsp cumin powder

14\. Cook with lid for another 2 minutes and turn off heat

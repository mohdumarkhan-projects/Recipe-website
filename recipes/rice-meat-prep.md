title: Rice \& Meat Prep

\## Ingredients



\* Tomato

\* Chicken

\* Milk

\* Potatoes (Aalu)

\* Cashews (Kaju)

\* Stock cube



\## Steps



1\. Cook the rice on high heat.

2\. Stir frequently with a spoon while cooking.

3\. Once the rice is fully cooked, drain all the water using a sieve.

4\. Add approximately 15 whole cashews to the masala.

5\. Check the tenderness of the meat pieces by lifting one from the bottom of the pot.

6\. Remove the cooked meat pieces and place them on a plate.

7\. Finish the final cooking stage on medium or low heat.



\## Notes



Ensure the stock cube is added to the base gravy. If shifting the pot to a larger burner, maintain medium or low heat.

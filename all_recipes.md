\## slug: bagara-chawal

title: Bagara Chawal (Spiced Rice)

image: bagara-chawal.jpg



\## Ingredients



\* Rice (soaked)

\* 3 tablespoons Oil

\* 5 tablespoons Ghee

\* 1 small Onion (thinly sliced)

\* 2 pinches Cumin seeds (Zeera)

\* 2 Green chilies (slit)

\* Coriander (Kothmir)

\* 8 leaves Mint (Podina)

\* Whole Garam Masalas

\* ½ teaspoon Ginger-Garlic Paste

\* Water

\* ½ tablespoon Salt



\## Steps



1\. Soak the rice and prepare all chopped ingredients.

2\. Heat 3 tablespoons oil and 5 tablespoons ghee in a pressure cooker.

3\. Sauté the thinly sliced onion until translucent.

4\. Add whole garam masala and half teaspoon ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.

5\. Stir in the mint, coriander, and green chilies.

6\. Add the soaked rice and mix well.

7\. Add water and half tablespoon salt and stir.

8\. Close the cooker and cook for 2 whistles.

9\. Release the pressure manually and turn off the gas.

\- Hot Red Chili Powder: Half tablespoon

\- Coriander Powder: 1 tablespoon

\- Cumin Powder: 1 tablespoon

\## Steps

2\. Heat mustard oil in a pot. Add washed chicken and sear for 5 minutes on a fast flame, turning the pieces.

3\. Add the entire ground paste to the chicken. Add 1 teaspoon of salt. Grind tomatoes into a puree and add to the pot.

\## Steps

1\. Cook the meat with ginger, garlic, and salt until tender.

2\. Add 1 cup of birista and whole garam masala to the meat.

3\. Add 1/2 cup oil and sauté for 5 minutes. Turn off gas and cover.

4\. Make a paste of 8 soaked cashews and 8 soaked almonds.

5\. Mix the paste with 200g yogurt, dried apricots, and some whole cashews.

6\. Grind 4 green cardamoms, 2 black cardamoms, and a small piece of nutmeg into a fine powder and add to the mixture.

7\. Add 1/2 cup cream and 1/2 cup mayonnaise. Add salt if needed.

8\. If using potatoes, fry them and mix into the meat mixture.

9\. Cook the entire mixture on slow gas flame for 10 minutes with 4 tablespoons of ghee, covered (stir once or twice).

12\. Place a thin layer of rice in the pot.

13\. Add the entire meat mixture and spread it out.

14\. Cover with the remaining rice.

15\. Sprinkle with biryani attar and kewra or rose water.

16\. Pour 2 tbsp ghee on top and seal for final cooking.
